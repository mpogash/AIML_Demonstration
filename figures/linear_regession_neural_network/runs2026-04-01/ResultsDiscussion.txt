
	Parametric evaluation of learning rate and number of epochs performed
		learning rate: 0.01, 0.1
		number of epochs: 10, 50, 100
		
	Notice that the TensorFlow model converges (with some bias, but not large) with 
	only 10 iterations with a learning rate of 0.1. The dataset is very well defined, 
	despite the noise. So the solution is easily found with a large learning rate. 
	
	Using a learning rate of 0.01 and 50 epochs, the TensorFlow model still does 
	not converge. 

	It is interesting to observe the collapse of the residuals and reduction of 
	the bias over the most ideal combination of learning rate and number of epochs
